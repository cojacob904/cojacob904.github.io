Favorite Games

Destiny

Destiny 1 and 2 have some of my favorite world building in all of gaming as well as offering a consistently fun multiplayer experience that always feels good when you finally get that exotic you've been grinding for, not to mention possibly my favorite video game experiences of all time which are their 6 player raids that encourage player cooperation at a difficulty level unlike any other!

Borderlands 2

Easily the best game in the Borderlands franchise the world is so fun and brutal at the same time and the humor always lands. A great game to play with friends and a great experience throughout, the guns feel truly amazing to use, each character class unique and fun, and a story that shines suprisingly bright given the unserious nature of this game.

The Last of Us 2

While The Last of Us: Part I is generally the more beloved game, I absolutely love Part II not only for its frankly phenomenal increase in gameplay quality from small details like NPCS begging for their life to the amazing scale of the battles and mechanics it creates, but also for its intense and grounded story that has you question the existence of right and wrong and the morals of both characters you play. Such an amazing and gut wrenching story, backed up by phenomenal gameplay, I think it is truly a shame more people did not enjoy this game as much as the first entry.

Hollow Knight: Silksong

Hollow Knight: Silksong absolutely nails it on visuals, soundtrack, world building, and gameplay, and is truly one of my favorite video game experiences in a long time. With a steep learning curve and a brutal world, success in this game truly feels earned and not many experiences compare to the flow state that is reached when you truly master a fight in this game. Each boss fight feels like a dance, perfectly choregraphed to the music creating an experience that I haven't found elsewhere. If you can get past the difficulty of learning this game it is an experience you truly won't forget.
